## 1. PURPOSE

The Parties wish to explore a potential business relationship involving AI-powered accounts payable automation and related services ("Purpose"). In connection with this Purpose, each Party may disclose Confidential Information to the other Party.

## 2. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

"Confidential Information" means any and all non-public information disclosed by either Party to the other, whether orally, in writing, electronically, or by inspection, including but not limited to:

**(a) n0v8v Confidential Information:**
- AI methods, algorithms, models, and frameworks
- Software tools, processes, and proprietary technology
- Business strategies, pricing models, and client lists
- Technical architecture and system designs
- Training data methodologies and prompt engineering techniques

**(b) Counterparty Confidential Information:**
- Company financial data, accounts payable records, and cash flow information
- Trade secrets, manufacturing processes, and proprietary business methods
- Customer lists, vendor relationships, and supplier agreements
- Employee information and organizational data
- Any business information shared during the engagement

## 3. OBLIGATIONS OF RECEIVING PARTY

Each Party, when acting as the Receiving Party, agrees to:

(a) Hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence;

(b) Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent of the Disclosing Party;

(c) Use Confidential Information solely for the Purpose stated herein;

(d) Restrict access to Confidential Information to employees, agents, and contractors who have a need to know and are bound by confidentiality obligations at least as protective as this Agreement;

(e) Notify the Disclosing Party promptly (within 24 hours) upon discovery of any unauthorized use or disclosure.

## 4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY RIGHTS

**(a) n0v8v Pre-Existing IP:** All AI methods, frameworks, tools, processes, algorithms, software, and proprietary technology developed by n0v8v prior to or independent of this engagement remain the exclusive property of n0v8v LLC. Nothing in this Agreement grants the Counterparty any rights to n0v8v's pre-existing intellectual property.

**(b) Counterparty Pre-Existing IP:** All intellectual property, trade secrets, business processes, and proprietary information owned by the Counterparty prior to this engagement remain the exclusive property of the Counterparty.

**(c) Jointly Developed IP:** Any intellectual property, custom solutions, or deliverables developed jointly by the Parties specifically for the Counterparty during the course of the engagement shall be the exclusive property of the Counterparty.

## 5. DATA SECURITY PROTOCOLS

Both Parties agree to implement and maintain the following security measures when handling the other Party's Confidential Information:

(a) **Encryption at Rest:** AES-256 encryption for all stored Confidential Information;

(b) **Encryption in Transit:** TLS 1.3 (or higher) for all data transmission;

(c) **Access Controls:** Role-based access controls limiting access to authorized personnel only;

(d) **Data Handling:** Secure processing environments with audit logging;

(e) **Data Disposal:** Secure deletion of Confidential Information upon termination of this Agreement or upon written request, with written confirmation of destruction within 30 days;

(f) **Incident Response:** Notification to the other Party within 24 hours of any suspected data breach or unauthorized access, with a written incident report within 72 hours.

## 6. EXCLUSIONS

Confidential Information does not include information that:

(a) Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Receiving Party;

(b) Was already known to the Receiving Party prior to disclosure, as documented by written records;

(c) Is independently developed by the Receiving Party without use of or reference to the Disclosing Party's Confidential Information;

(d) Is received from a third party without restriction and without breach of any obligation of confidentiality.

## 7. COMPELLED DISCLOSURE

If the Receiving Party is compelled by law, regulation, or legal process to disclose Confidential Information, the Receiving Party shall (a) provide prompt written notice to the Disclosing Party (to the extent legally permitted), (b) cooperate with the Disclosing Party's efforts to obtain protective treatment, and (c) disclose only the minimum information required.

## 8. TERM AND TERMINATION

(a) This Agreement shall remain in effect for a period of **two (2) years** from the Effective Date unless terminated earlier by either Party upon 30 days' written notice.

(b) The confidentiality obligations set forth herein shall survive termination of this Agreement for a period of **three (3) years** following termination.

## 9. REMEDIES

Each Party acknowledges that any breach of this Agreement may cause irreparable harm for which monetary damages would be an inadequate remedy. Accordingly, the non-breaching Party shall be entitled to seek equitable relief, including injunction and specific performance, in addition to any other remedies available at law or in equity.

## 10. GENERAL PROVISIONS

(a) **Governing Law:** This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Ohio, without regard to its conflicts of law principles.

(b) **Entire Agreement:** This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties regarding the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior agreements, whether written or oral.

(c) **Amendments:** This Agreement may only be amended by a written instrument signed by both Parties.

(d) **Severability:** If any provision of this Agreement is held invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

(e) **No Waiver:** Failure to enforce any provision of this Agreement shall not constitute a waiver of the right to enforce that provision or any other provision.

(f) **Assignment:** Neither Party may assign this Agreement without the prior written consent of the other Party.
